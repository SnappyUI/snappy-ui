## Props

| **Name**             | **Type**     | **Default**       | **Description**                                              |
|----------------------|--------------|-------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| `buttonLabel`        | `string`     | "Feedback"        | The label on the feedback trigger button                     |
| `placeholder`        | `string`     | "Feedback"        | Placeholder text inside the textarea                         |
| `successTitle`       | `string`     | "Feedback received!" | Title shown after successful submission                    |
| `successDescription` | `string`     | "Thanks for helping me improve this." | Message under the success title              |
