## Props

| <b>Name</b>        | <b>Type</b>          | <b>Description</b>                              |
|------------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| **text**  | string      | You can highlight your Text(choose from effect from SnappyText,ColourfulText,OceanText, SunsetText,NeonText,MonochromeText,ForestText,GalaxyText,CyberpunkText,VintageText,<br/> CandyText,MatrixText,PastelText)                   |
